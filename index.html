My Top Animanga

1. Steins;Gate

Steins;Gate is a fantastic anime that tells a complex and engaging story. The characters are well-developed and the plot is both thought-provoking and emotionally impactful. Overall, it is a must-watch for any fan of science fiction and drama. I highly recommend it!

2. One Piece

One Piece is an outstanding anime that has it all: action, adventure, humor, and heart. The story follows the journey of Monkey D. Luffy and his friends as they search for the legendary treasure known as One Piece. The world-building is incredible and the characters are incredibly likable and relatable. The animation is top-notch and the fight scenes are some of the best in anime. I have been following this series for a long time and it never fails to impress me.

3. Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is an incredible anime that tells a powerful and compelling story. The historical setting is expertly researched and rendered, giving the audience a deep understanding of the culture and conflicts of the time. The characters are complex and well-developed, with motivations that are both relatable and understandable. The story is both brutal and moving and will keep you on the edge of your seat. I highly recommend Vinland Saga to anyone who is a fan of historical fiction and action. It's a must-watch.

4. Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is an anime that not only delivers on intense action and thrilling plot, but also on building an incredibly detailed and complex world. The world-building is masterful and the history, politics, and social structures are all intricately woven together to create a believable and fully-realized world. The emotional value of the show also adds a whole other level of depth, as the characters are forced to confront not only the Titans but also the moral and ethical dilemmas that come with survival.

5. Monster

Monster is a psychological thriller anime that tells the story of Kenzou Tenma, a highly skilled neurosurgeon who faces moral dilemmas when he saves the life of a young boy instead of a mayor. The story takes place in a world where politics and power play a significant role and it's filled with intricate plot twists and turns.
